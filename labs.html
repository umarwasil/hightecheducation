INSIDE HIGH TECH LABS

A Playground for Builders

Humari labs sirf computer rooms nahi hain — yahan har system, headset, board aur device is waiting for you to **experiment**. No “sir just show the output and move on” culture.

You get access to dedicated **practice slots**, **project hours** and **guided lab sessions** aligned with your course. Seat milna hi nahi, machine milna bhi guaranteed hota hai.

  • Separate labs for Web, AI, Robotics & VR so that you get the right environment for each skill.
  • Lab assistants & mentors available during peak hours to help you debug & build faster.
  • Special **Project Nights** and **Hackathon Saturdays** for intense building sprints.

ACCESS & TIMINGS

Lab Hours & Access Policy

Hum chahte hain ki aapko machine ke liye wait na karna pade. Isliye lab access **planned & organised** hota hai. Har student ko fair time milta hai.

Regular Lab Timings

  • Mon–Sat: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM
  • Batch-wise dedicated lab slots
  • Extra hours during project weeks

Extended Access*

  • Special late-night access for hackathons
  • Final-year / capstone projects priority
  • *As per institute schedule & approval

Lab Etiquette & Safety

  • ✔ Respect hardware – treat every machine as your own.
  • ✔ No food & drinks near systems / electronics.
  • ✔ Use your **own GitHub** and cloud accounts for projects.
  • ✔ Follow mentor instructions during VR/Robotics sessions for safety.

Lab Access Pass

Lab access is linked with your **course enrollment**. But serious builders can also apply for **Lab Membership**.

  • ✔ Included access with all flagship programs
  • ✔ Extra lab hours for project teams
  • ✔ Priority booking for hackathon teams

Want extended access?

Talk to your mentor or the lab coordinator about **Project Membership**. Yahan se best portfolios nikalte hain.
